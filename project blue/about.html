About

This is a web based student management system .It has two sides the admin staff and students side.Its functions include amongst others -

  • Online payment of school tuition
  • Teacher and Student/Parent Interaction
  • Issuing of Student Reports
  • Enrollment of new students
  • Issuing of reports
  • Student forums
  • Timetables
  • Calenders
  • Notices
  • Enquiries
  • Curriculum ;-amongst others

Why use it?

  • The need for paper based student files is removed
  • Ease of enrollment and registration for the students
  • Handling of all applications
  • Student and teacher interaction after school
  • Updates of all school past and present events are all a fingertip away
  • Feedback and suggestions
